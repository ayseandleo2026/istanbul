Venue & Travel

How to get there, and what helps.

Venue

Fuat Pasa Oteli

Right on the Bosphorus, in Sarıyer, the venue sits between the water and the hills. It’s calm, green, and a perfect Istanbul backdrop for the night.

Open in Google Maps Venue website

Arriving

Airports and transfers

  • Airports: Istanbul Airport (IST) and Sabiha Gökçen (SAW)
  • Taxi apps: BiTaksi, iTaksi, Uber (taxi)
  • Metro: IST: M11 to Gayrettepe + connect to the metro network. SAW: M4 metro from the airport.
Timing

Typical drive time is around 1h to 1h30 depending on traffic. Plan extra time during rush hours.

Tips

Good to know

We’ll keep this section updated with practical tips from the venue.

  • Do: leave a bit of extra time for traffic
  • Do: bring comfortable shoes for walking
  • Avoid: last-minute transfer planning

Where to stay

A few premium zones, plus options close to the venue.

Recommended zones

Pick what fits your trip

  • Nişantaşı (premium, calm, shopping) · ~35–55 min to Fuat Pasa
  • Galata / Karaköy (central, restaurants, easy to explore) · ~45–70 min to Fuat Pasa
  • Bosphorus line (Bebek / Arnavutköy) (very premium, beautiful) · ~30–55 min to Fuat Pasa
  • Fuat Pasa Oteli (on-site) · 0 min
  • Tarabya / Yeniköy (close to the venue) · ~10–25 min to Fuat Pasa
  • İstinye / Emirgan (close to the venue) · ~15–30 min to Fuat Pasa

Dress code

Keep it elegant, comfortable, and you.

Style

Think elevated evening. Dark tones, earthy tones, and timeless pieces always work well.

Colors

Navy, black, deep greens, browns, and soft neutrals.

Practical

Comfort matters
