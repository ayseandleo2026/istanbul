Arriving

Airports, transfers, and timing

  • Airports: IST (Istanbul Airport) and SAW (Sabiha Gökçen)
  • Taxi apps: BiTaksi, iTaksi, Uber
  • Metro: M11 from IST connects toward Gayrettepe. M4 connects SAW toward Kadıköy
  • Typical travel time: 1h to 1h30 (traffic dependent)
Traffic can change a lot depending on the time. If you can, plan a bit of buffer.
