Venue

Fuat Pasa Oteli

A calm place above the Bosphorus, with Istanbul all around you. A hotel that feels like a viewpoint.

Open in Google Maps Venue website

Arriving

  • IST (Istanbul Airport)
  • SAW (Sabiha Gokcen)
  • Taxi apps: BiTaksi, Uber, iTaksi
  • Metro: available from both airports (route depends on your area)
  • Typical travel time: 1h to 1h30 (traffic dependent)

Tips

If the hotel publishes any specific guidance, we’ll add it here. Otherwise we’ll share a short list closer to the date.

  • Plan extra buffer for traffic
  • Bring comfortable shoes
  • Keep cash for small expenses

Top zones

  • Nisantasi (premium, shopping) · ~35–55 min to Fuat Pasa
  • Bebek / Arnavutkoy (Bosphorus) · ~15–25 min to Fuat Pasa
  • Karakoy / Galata (central, walkable) · ~35–60 min to Fuat Pasa
